## Ground Transportation

| Segment | Recommendation |
|---------|----------------|

| Toluca region | **Rent a car** - needed for volcano |

## Packing Checklist

### For Toluca & San José (High Altitude/Cool)

- [ ] Warm layers (mornings/evenings cool)
- [ ] Light jacket or fleece
- [ ] Warm hat (especially for Nevado)
- [ ] Comfortable walking shoes
- [ ] Motion sickness medication

### For Oaxaca City (Warm)

- [ ] Light, breathable clothing
- [ ] Comfortable walking shoes
- [ ] Sun hat, sunglasses
- [ ] Light scarf (for churches)

### For Beach

- [ ] Swimsuits (2-3)
- [ ] Reef-safe sunscreen
- [ ] Sandals and water shoes
- [ ] Very light clothing
- [ ] Snorkel gear (optional - can rent)

### General

- [ ] Passport
- [ ] Pesos cash (especially for San José - no ATMs!)
- [ ] Spanish phrasebook/app
- [ ] Daypack for excursions

## February/March Weather

| Location | Conditions |
|----------|------------|
| **Toluca** | Cool and dry. Days ~65-70°F, nights ~40°F. Bring layers. |
| **Oaxaca City** | Warm and dry. ~80°F days, ~55°F nights. Perfect. |
| **San José del Pacífico** | Cool, misty. ~60-70°F. Bring warm layers for evenings. |
| **Mazunte/Zipolite** | Hot and dry. ~90°F days, ~75°F nights. Beach weather. |
