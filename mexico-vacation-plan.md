# Mexico Vacation Plan: Toluca Region + Pacific Beach

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|

---

## Destination Profiles

### Toluca & Surroundings

- **Location**: Capital of State of Mexico
- **Elevation**: ~8,750 ft (cooler climate - pack layers!)
- **Character**: Colonial architecture, indigenous heritage, gateway to natural wonders

**Key Attractions**:
- Cosmovitral Botanical Garden (stunning stained glass)
- Nevado de Toluca volcano (Sun & Moon crater lagoons)
- Calixtlahuaca archaeological ruins
- Valle de Bravo (lakeside town, adventure activities)

### Beach Destination Comparison

| Aspect | Zihuatanejo | Puerto Vallarta | Sayulita |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|----------|
| **Vibe** | Romantic, peaceful | Cosmopolitan, diverse | Bohemian, surfer |
| **Size** | Small fishing town | Large city (220k+) | Small town (~3k) |
| **Flight from Toluca** | ~1 hr direct (TLC→ZIH) | ~1.5 hrs (may connect via GDL) | 45 min from PVR airport |
| **Best For** | Fishing, relaxation, authentic Mexico | Variety, nightlife, LGBTQ+ friendly | Surfing, yoga, laid-back |
| **Beaches** | 4 beautiful bays | 42km coastline, many options | One main + nearby coves |

**Recommendation**: **Zihuatanejo** - closer to Toluca, less touristy, stunning beaches, authentic coastal experience. Choose Puerto Vallarta if you prefer more variety/nightlife.

#### Day 4: Volcano & Ruins + Flight to Beach
| Time | Activity | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 5:00 AM | Depart for Nevado de Toluca | Early start essential |

#### Ground Transportation
| Segment | Recommendation |
|---------|----------------|
| Toluca region | **Rent a car** - essential for Nevado de Toluca, Valle de Bravo, flexibility |
| Toluca → Beach | **Fly** - 1 hr flight vs. 5-6 hr drive; return rental car at TLC or MEX |
| At beach | Taxis sufficient in Zihuatanejo; no car needed |

### Packing Checklist

**For Toluca (High Altitude)**:
- [ ] Layers (mornings/evenings cool, ~50-65°F)
- [ ] Light jacket or fleece
- [ ] Comfortable walking shoes
- [ ] Warm hat and gloves (for Nevado de Toluca hike)
- [ ] Sunscreen (high altitude = stronger UV)

**For Beach**:
- [ ] Swimsuits (2-3)
- [ ] Reef-safe sunscreen
- [ ] Sandals and water shoes
- [ ] Light, breathable clothing
- [ ] Hat and sunglasses
- [ ] Snorkel gear (optional - can rent)

**General**:
- [ ] Passport
- [ ] Pesos (ATMs available, but have some cash)
- [ ] Spanish phrasebook/app
- [ ] Motion sickness meds (winding mountain roads)
- [ ] Daypack for excursions

## Decision Points

Before finalizing, consider:

1. **Beach choice**: Zihuatanejo (peaceful, authentic) vs. Puerto Vallarta (vibrant, varied)?
2. **Valle de Bravo**: Day trip or overnight stay?
3. **Activity level**: Pack the itinerary or leave room for spontaneity?
4. **Dates**: Specific timing affects prices, weather, and crowds
