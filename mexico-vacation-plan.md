## Destination Profiles

### Toluca & Surroundings

- **Location**: Capital of State of Mexico, 1 hr from Mexico City
- **Elevation**: ~8,750 ft (cooler climate - pack layers!)
- **Character**: Colonial architecture, indigenous heritage, gateway to natural wonders
- **Purpose**: Acclimate to altitude before Oaxaca adventure

**Key Attractions**:
- Cosmovitral Botanical Garden (stunning stained glass)
- Nevado de Toluca volcano (Sun & Moon crater lagoons)
- Calixtlahuaca archaeological ruins
- Amaranta restaurant (Latin America's 50 Best)

### Oaxaca City

- **Location**: Southern Mexico, Oaxaca state capital
- **Elevation**: ~5,085 ft (lower than Toluca - you'll feel great!)
- **Character**: UNESCO World Heritage, indigenous cultures, food capital of Mexico
- **Vibe**: Artsy, culinary, mezcal-soaked, vibrant markets

**Key Attractions**:
- Monte Alb√°n (Zapotec ruins)
- Mercado Benito Ju√°rez & 20 de Noviembre
- Mezcal tastings (In Situ, La Mezcaloteca)
- Hierve el Agua (petrified waterfalls)

### San Jos√© del Pac√≠fico

- **Location**: Mountain village between Oaxaca City and coast
- **Elevation**: ~8,200 ft (you'll be acclimated by now)
- **Character**: Mystical cloud forest, disconnection, healing
- **Drive**: 3 hrs from Oaxaca City, 3.5 hrs from coast

**What It's Known For**:
- Cloud inversions and epic sunsets
- Temazcal ceremonies (traditional sweat lodge)
- Hiking through pine forests
- Complete disconnection (limited wifi/cell service)
- Magic mushrooms (season is July-Oct, not Feb/March)

### Mazunte & Zipolite (Oaxaca Coast)

- **Location**: Pacific coast, ~6 hrs from Oaxaca City
- **Vibe**: Bohemian, relaxed, yoga retreats, clothing-optional (Zipolite)
- **Best For**: Unwinding after the mountains, sunsets, fresh seafood

**The Beaches**:
| Beach | Vibe | Best For |
|-------|------|----------|
| **Mazunte** | Chill, hippie, yoga | Families, couples, sunsets at Punta Cometa |
| **Zipolite** | Bohemian, nude-friendly | Freedom, backpackers, El Alquimista restaurant |
| **San Agustinillo** | Quieter, between the two | Swimming, mellow surf, walking distance to both |
| **Playa Estacahuite** | Small cove, turquoise | Snorkeling, dramatic cliffs |
